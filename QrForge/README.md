## 🎯 Why QR Forge?

We built QR Forge because creating QR codes shouldn't mean:

- ❌ Hunting for the perfect online tool
- ❌ Uploading your data to random servers
- ❌ Being limited to boring, generic designs
- ❌ Struggling with inflexible export options

**QR Forge changes that.** It's a fully-featured, open-source QR code generator built by developers, for developers. Everything runs in your browser. No servers. No tracking. No BS.

## ✨ Features

### 📝 Multi-Format Content

Generate QR codes for anything:

- **URL** => Link anyone to your content
- **Text** => Encode plain messages
- **Email** => Auto-fill recipient, subject and body
- **Wi-Fi** => Share network credentials (WPA, WEP)
- **vCard** => Encode contact information (name, phone, email, company, website)

### 🎨 Unlimited Customization

Make your QR codes _yours_:

- **6 dot styles** => Square, Rounded, Dots, Classy, Classy Rounded, Extra Rounded
- **3 corner square styles** => Square, Rounded, Dot
- **Full color control** => Customize dots, background and corner colors
- **Color presets** => Classic, Ocean, Forest, Grape, Ember, Night (or create your own)
- **Fine-tuned sizing** => QR size (200–600px) and quiet zone margins (0–40px)

### 🖼️ Embedded Center Image

Add logos or icons to the center of your QR code:

- Support for PNG, SVG, JPEG and more
- **Transparent PNG recommended** for best results
- Adjustable size (10–40% of QR code)
- Multiple background styles => Transparent, White circle or Match QR
- Smart error correction handling

### 📊 Error Correction Levels

Choose how robust your QR code should be:

- **L (Low)** => 7% recovery capacity
- **M (Medium)** => 15% recovery capacity (default)
- **Q (Quartile)** => 25% recovery capacity
- **H (High)** => 30% recovery capacity (recommended with logos)

### 💾 Multi-Format Export

Download in the format that fits your use case:

- **PNG** => Raster format for web & print
- **SVG** => Infinitely scalable vector graphics
- **JPEG** => Small file size with compression
- **WEBP** => Modern, highly compressed format

Export sizes: 512×512, 1024×1024, 2048×2048 or custom dimensions up to 4000×4000px.

### 🔍 Live Preview & Metadata

- Real-time preview as you customize
- View encoded data length, QR version and error correction level
- Test scannability directly from your phone

## 🚀 How It Works

QR Forge is **100% client-side** => no server, no API calls, no tracking:

1. **You create** → Configure your QR code content and styling
2. **We render** → Live preview updates instantly as you customize
3. **You download** → Export in any format without leaving your browser

All processing happens locally on your device. Your data never leaves your machine.

---

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

- **Adding a logo?** Use _Error Correction Level H_ to ensure scannability
- **Transparent backgrounds?** Choose PNG or SVG for best results
- **Mobile sharing?** Export as SVG for lossless scaling
- **Print projects?** PNG at 2048×2048 ensures crisp output
